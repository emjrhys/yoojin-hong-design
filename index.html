  • Primitive Magazine | Mobile and Print

    Primitive Magazine

    Mobile | Print

    Primitive is a fictional magazine that I conceptualized and designed while exploring both print and mobile design. My goal for Primitive Magazine was to create a cohesive design through the two platforms.

    I included an essay called "Design Invisible Century" by Thomas Fisher and a gallery from the blog "Mirror Mirror." The pages were printed, cut, and bound at the UIUC Print Lab.
